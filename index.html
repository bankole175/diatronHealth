In Africa

  • Chronic condition care is poorly coordinated, at the primary and secondary public health care centres.

  • Over 30% are living with undetected chronic condition and even for those that are diagnosed, just a fraction own and control their health

  • Managing chronic condition is beyond reach for an average African

  • It is estimated that 19million people(20 - 79) are living with Diabetes and the number keeps increasing daily as the environment keeps evolving

  • Chronic condition treatment is not personalized

  • Lack of periodic test, lack of data for personalized treatment, late detection has lead to many deaths

Why Diatron Glucometer

Unlike a traditional glucometer where after test is taken user do not have access to their record again. Diatron glucometer allow you to:

  • Own your health
  • Know the food/exercise/activity that triggers your blood sugar level to increase or decrease
  • How other factors, such as illness or stress, affect blood sugar levels
  • Take away the risk of assumption which has led to lot of untimely death
  • Make personalized decision
  • Live happier, celebrate more, cut your healthcare cost

When you check your blood pressure, the result is stored, the result is used to know what to do and not to do, shared with loved ones, health practitioner, for personalized monitoring/treatment of chronic conditions.

Why Diatron Scale

Being overweight or obese increases a person's risk for developing type 2 diabetes. Also, weight gain in people with type 2 diabetes makes blood sugar levels even harder to control. With Diatron scale we can:

  • Keep our blood sugar in balance, live healthier and have more energy
  • Decrease our chances of developing complications from Diabetes like heart disease
  • Know when to keep the pounds off and when not to
  • Take away the risk of assumption
  • Make personalized decision
  • Live happier, celebrate more, cut your healthcare cost

When you check your body weight, the result is stored, the result is used to know what to do and not to do, shared with loved ones, health practitioner, for personalized monitoring/treatment.
